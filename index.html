About Me

Hi! My Name is Luciana I am Junior Frontend Software Developer originally from Peru , two years ago I decided I wanted a new challenge and decided to move from Peru to Germany and become a Software Developer, I am always looking to improve and learn, lovee team work and to apport ideas. beside software another passion is Dancing, I love Salsa and Bachatas. Now I will tell you about my Journey

When I first got to Germany I started doing a 10 months online Bootcamp with OpenClassroom and finshed the Frontend Path, after that did a React Udacity Nanodegree, and right after got a three months Intership by the Company Wiedergrün where I got the chance two use what I learned in React and builded the company Product Website you can have a look here

After finishing the intership with Wiedergrün decided to look for something a little bit more exciting and joined BRYTER in Frankfurt am Main where I worked as a Frontend Software Developer and learned from scratch to work with Vue.js, typescript and jest, the Team was amazing and I am so grateful I got to work with them.

Currently I decided to focus more on my self learning and to get my German language better also to start writting tech articles, I must say the main reason why I programm is because I love to see the impact that my work can have on others people lives. something really amazing is that when you feel motivated you tend to learn more in a short amount of time.
